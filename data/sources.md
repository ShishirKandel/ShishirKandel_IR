# Training Data Sources

The training corpus is stored in `data/training_documents.csv`.

## Dataset Summary

| Category | Document Count |
|----------|----------------|
| BUSINESS | 955 |
| ENTERTAINMENT | 955 |
| HEALTH | 955 |
| **Total** | **2,865** |

## Data Format

CSV file with two columns:
- `category`: Classification label (BUSINESS, ENTERTAINMENT, HEALTH)
- `text`: Preprocessed document text

## Source

The training data consists of news article excerpts covering three domains:
- **Business**: Financial news, corporate announcements, market analysis
- **Entertainment**: Film, music, celebrity news, awards coverage
- **Health**: Medical research, healthcare policy, wellness topics
